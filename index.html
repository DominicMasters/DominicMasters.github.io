Saturday

Our wedding ceremony will take place on Saturday 1st July 2017 at 1:30pm.
Please arrive at the venue from 12:30pm and take a seat in the ceremony area in the woods.

Evening guests – Please arrive at 6pm.

Note: If you are camping in Munday’s Meadow, please see ‘Accommodation’ section below for arrival times.

The order of the day:

12:30pm – 1:15pm : Arrival of day guests
1:30pm: Wedding Ceremony
2pm: Reception drinks and live music from Buffalo Skinners
4pm: Picnic wedding breakfast in the marquee
6pm: Arrival of evening guests
8.30pm: Taco Van
8.30pm – Late: Party time!

Dress Code
Our wedding will be a relaxed and fun-filled day, and we want our guests to feel comfortable! Smart casual clothes are fine, as are more traditional wedding outfits and hats. Please do note that our venue is almost entirely outdoors, so if you are planning to wear heels then you may wish to consider some heel protectors!

Drinks
We will be providing plenty of drinks throughout the day and into the evening, however, in the spirit of sharing and caring, we would be very grateful if you could 'bring a bottle' to add to the collection!

Sunday

If you are able to, we would love for you to stay with us until Sunday. We will be having a BBQ and sports day and expect maximum effort on the ‘sports kit’ front – think retro sports kit, 80’s theme etc; as long as you commit to the outfit, we will be happy!

We ask that if you wish to stay for Sunday, please bring something to contribute to the BBQ and any alcohol you might want.

The day will begin at 11am and end at 5pm when we are required to vacate the site.

The venue is situated midway between Moreton-in-Marsh and Stow on the Wold.

Driving:
The venue is approximately 1 hour drive from Birmingham and 1 hour 20 mins from Bristol.
• From Stow on the Wold, continue on the Fosseway passing Tesco's on your right. After approximately 1.5 miles you will see signs to Donnington (left hand side) and Broadwell (right hand side).
• Continue on main road and take 3rd driveway entrance on the right hand side marked WildWood Bluebell and Heath Barn. Do not take the 2nd entrance with the wrought iron gates.
• Please take care on this main road as it is very busy. Make sure you are also careful when departing the driveway, as cars are often overtaking on this stretch of road.

Unlimited parking is available on site in the field adjacent to the barn.

Train:
There is a train station in Moreton-In-Marsh which is 2.4 miles from our venue. There are regular trains from London, Birmingham, Bristol and Sheffield to Moreton-in-Marsh.

Taxi:
If you are staying in a hotel nearby, it may be advisable to pre-book a taxi for the time you expect to leave.

Accomodation

There are a few options for accommodation:

Camping
Our venue has a dedicated camping area called Munday’s Meadow, where we will be staying for the duration of the weekend.
There are two camping options:

• Hiring a bell tent: We have hired in range of beautiful furnished bell tents (pictured) so all of you can camp in comfort. They are 4m in diameter and come with either a double or two single blow up beds as well as a carpet, bedding, pillows, lighting, decorations and a table.
These will be £150 per tent.
Up to 2 extra single beds can also be added for an additional £25 each.

• Bring your own tent: If you wish to bring your own tent rather than hire a bell tent, the cost of this will be £20 a night pitch fee.
If you are bringing your own tent and arriving for Saturday day time, you will need to arrive on site in plenty of time to pitch your tent and get ready.
We would advise being on site for around 10am.

If you are coming for just the evening reception please arrive from 6pm.

Hotel, BnB’s and Air BnB’s
We hope that as many of you as possible will join us in camping but if staying in the great outdoors isn’t your thing then that’s absolutely fine! There are lots of lovely hotels and Air BnB’s in Moreton in Marsh and Stow on the Wold for you to enjoy instead.
